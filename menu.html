ZAUQ MENU

At Zauq Indian Cuisine, we don’t cut corners when it comes to flavour and authenticity. Our spices are carefully roasted and ground in-house every week to create the rich aromas and bold tastes that Indian cuisine is famous for. Every curry, biryani, kebab, and tandoori dish is prepared using traditional family recipes passed down through generations from the kitchens of Delhi and Punjab — never pre-made sauces or artificial flavourings. We believe great food takes time, passion, and authentic ingredients. From creamy butter chicken and fragrant lamb biryani to sizzling kebabs and freshly baked naan, every dish on our menu is crafted to deliver the true taste of India. Our chefs combine traditional cooking methods with fresh, high-quality ingredients to create meals that are full of depth, warmth, and unforgettable flavour. Whether you enjoy mild and creamy dishes or bold and spicy flavours, the Zauq menu offers something for everyone. Every bite tells a story of tradition, culture, and passion for authentic Indian cooking. At Zauq Indian Cuisine, you don’t just eat Indian food — you experience the real India.
